What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is understanding your own emotions and other people's too. Managing how you react, staying aware of what you’re feeling, and handling working relationships in a suitable manner.

How is EQ different to IQ?

IQ is about logic, problem solving, and how fast you learn new info. EQ is more about emotions which can be shown by how you handle stress, communicate, and work with others. You can have a high IQ but low EQ or the other way around.

Why is EQ important?

Because tech isn’t just solo work, you’ve gotta collaborate. Even if you’re skilled, poor communication or stress can be the biggest roadblock when making projects. Plus, startups (like where I wanna go) need people who adapt fast and work well in teams.
